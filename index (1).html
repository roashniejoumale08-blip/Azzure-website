Our Story

Cooked slow.
Served simply.

Ember & Salt started as a small weekend pop-up in a parking lot. We had one wood-burning barrel, a folding table, and too much ambition. People kept coming back, so we built a proper kitchen — but kept the same barrel.

Everything on our menu changes with what's good at the market that week. We work with five local farms and one very reliable fisherman named Gerald.

🔥

"We don't use a timer. We use our eyes, our nose, and six years of practice."
